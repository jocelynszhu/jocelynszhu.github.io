Jocelyn Zhu

I am a senior studying Computation and Cognition at MIT. Currently, I work with several labs across the Institute in Computational Social Research, including the Human Cooperation Lab and Center for Constructive Communication..

Previously, I have interned at Native Design in London as an AI Technology Intern, where I researched and developed NLP applications for consumer-tech. As a Summer@EPFL Fellow, I researched task-agnostic, few-shot learning implementations for Biomedical Data at the MLBio Lab in Lausanne, Switzerland.
